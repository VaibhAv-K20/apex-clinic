What We Treat

Comprehensive care for the whole family.

From your newborn's first check-up to managing your parents' chronic conditions — we handle it all under one roof, with the same familiar face.

01

General Consultations

Fever, infections, allergies, respiratory illness, and anything that's worrying you. Same-day appointments usually available for acute cases.

02

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term management of diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders, heart disease, and COPD with personalised treatment plans and regular monitoring.

03

Family Health Check-ups

Periodic preventive health screenings for all age groups — blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol panels, and age-appropriate tests.

04

Specialist Referrals

When your care needs a specialist, we coordinate referrals with Nagpur's top hospitals and maintain continuity so nothing falls through the cracks.

05

Geriatric & Senior Care

Tailored care for older adults with multiple health concerns — medication reviews, fall risk assessment, and compassionate chronic pain management.

06

Preventive Wellness

Lifestyle counselling, vaccinations, nutrition guidance, and health risk assessments to keep you healthy before illness strikes.
